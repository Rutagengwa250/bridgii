About Us

We are BRIDGII. We bridge the gap in education systems through the provision of visualized learning materials including 3D models, simulations, virtual labs, and AI-driven video content aligned with the Rwandan CBC (Competency Based Curriculum).

Mission

BRIDGII brings 3D visualization, simulations and AI-driven content to every classroom in Kigali.

Vision

We aim at producing high quality visualistic 3D models and simulations created by ourselves.
